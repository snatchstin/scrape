How is the APY calculated?
APY stands for Annual Percentage Yield. It is a percentage that signifies the rate of rewards over the course of a year, taking into account compounding. The APY of the vault changes depending on how many people are entering and exiting the vault. The APY listed on the top of the ELONGATE vault represents the average APY from the last 7 days. It is also important to note that APY is not the same as APR. If you were to take the current rate and divide it by 365, that would NOT give you the daily APY as that does not take compounding into account. APR tends to be significantly lower due to not taking compounding into account.

Kyle | CSS
In reply to this message
APY stands for Annual Percentage Yield. It is a percentage that signifies the rate of rewards over the course of a year taking into account compounding. The APY of the vault changes depending on how many people are entering and exiting the vault. The APY that can be seen at the top of the ELONGATE vault represents the average APY from the last 7 days. It is also important to note that APY is not the same thing as APR. If you were to take the current rate and divide it by 365, that would NOT give you the daily APY as that does not take compounding into account. APR tends to be significantly lower due to not taking compounding into account. For example, an APR of 400% compounding daily leads to an APY of 5242%.
